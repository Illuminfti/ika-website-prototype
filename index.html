How it works

Your Solana program.
Any chain's key.

Ika gives your smart contract a programmable MPC wallet that can sign on any chain. No bridges. No wrapping. No trust. Just sub-second cryptographic signing across 1,000+ non-collusive nodes.

Massively Decentralised

Scale to hundreds and thousands of signer nodes without performance degradation. No more 4-8 node ceilings.

Sub-Second Latency

While legacy networks lag 30+ seconds, Ika operates at sub-second speeds. Real-time signing for real-time DeFi.

Zero-Trust Security

2PC-MPC ensures no subset of validators can collude. Your assets stay secure even in worst-case scenarios.

Native Multi-Chain

Sign on Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, Sui, and any ECDSA chain. No bridges. No wrapped assets. True native interop.

10,000x Throughput
