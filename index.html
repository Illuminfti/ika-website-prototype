— How It Works

Three steps to
universal signing

01

Create a dWallet

Generate a distributed wallet. The private key is never held by any single party — it exists only as shares across the network.

02

Write your logic

Define signing policies in Move. Gate transactions by on-chain state, multi-party approval, time locks, or any composable condition.

03

Sign on any chain

Submit signatures to Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and every supported chain. Sub-second finality. No bridges, no trust assumptions.
