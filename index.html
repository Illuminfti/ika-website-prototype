The Problem

Cross-chain is fundamentally broken.

Bridges get hacked. Wrapped assets introduce systemic risk. Legacy MPC is slow and centralized. Builders deserve a better primitive for a multi-chain world.

Zero-Trust Security

Utilizing advanced 2PC-MPC, Ika ensures no subset of validators can collude to forge a signature, eliminating single points of failure.

Sub-Second Speed

Engineered for high-frequency DeFi and real-time applications, Ika signs transactions in under a second, a 100x improvement over legacy systems.

Massive Decentralization

Our network scales horizontally to thousands of non-collusive nodes, providing unparalleled resilience and censorship resistance.

Chain Agnostic

From Bitcoin to Solana to any future chain, Ika provides a universal signing layer, no bridges or wrapping required.

Programmable Custody

Define complex signing logic, spending limits, and recovery mechanisms directly within your smart contracts for ultimate control.

Gas Abstraction

Sponsor transactions for your users on any chain, creating seamless, gas-less experiences from a single point of control.
